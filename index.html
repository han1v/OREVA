What is OREVA OCR?

OREVA OCR is a free, browser-based Optical Character Recognition tool that converts non-searchable images and PDFs into searchable and editable text.

How It Works

  1. Upload an image or PDF
  2. OCR runs securely on your device
  3. Text is extracted and embedded
  4. Download searchable files instantly

Supported Files

  • Images (JPG, PNG, scanned photos)
  • PDFs (scanned / non-searchable)

Privacy First

Files never leave your device. No uploads, no storage, no tracking of content. Only anonymous analytics are used to improve performance.
